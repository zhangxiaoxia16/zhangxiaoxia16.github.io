Discussion

We regularly discuss interesting topics among our department’s faculty and students. The goal is to have a comprehensive review of these topics. For each topic, we will meet once a week and discuss all the relevant literature, including review articles, the latest discoveries, etc. We expect all participants will actively participate in reading and discussing processes.
